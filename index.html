When customers try to cancel, they often just need a break

Before You Cancel is a Shopify app that lets customers delay their next shipment instead of cancelling — saving subscriptions at the exact moment they would otherwise be lost.

Fewer cancellations. Fewer support tickets. More retained revenue.

  • Customers delay their next shipment instead of cancelling
  • No coupons, no negotiations, no support tickets
  • Works with your existing subscription setup
  • Installs in minutes with no impact on checkout

Why subscriptions get cancelled

Most customers don't cancel because they're done forever. They cancel because:

  • They have too much product right now
  • They want to pause spending temporarily
  • They don't want to talk to support

When the only obvious option is to cancel, that's what they choose — even when a short pause would have solved the problem.

What you'll see

  • An immediate analysis of when churn happens when you install the app
  • Simple performance metrics (emails sent, clicks, saves)
  • Predictable pricing based on your current scale

You'll always know where you stand — and you'll be notified before any plan change.
