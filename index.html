Why subscriptions get cancelled (and why it feels out of your control)

Most customers don't cancel because they're done forever.

They cancel because:

  • They have too much product right now
  • They want to pause spending temporarily
  • They forgot they were subscribed
  • They don't want to talk to support

When the only option is Cancel, that's what they choose — even when delay would have solved the problem.

What Before You Cancel does

Before You Cancel adds a Delay delivery option directly into your existing cancellation flow.

When a subscriber clicks "Cancel", they're first offered a simple alternative:

  • Delay the next shipment
  • Keep their subscription active
  • No coupons, no negotiations, no tickets

You keep the customer.
They avoid an unwanted cancellation.
